BetterFinance App

BetterFinance is a robust web application designed for logged-in users to engage in various financial activities. Users have the ability to "buy & sell" stocks, "deposit & withdraw" demo money, and update or reset their login details. The application fetches real-time stock quotes from the IEX API, ensuring accurate data for informed decision-making. Additionally, users can conveniently monitor their stock portfolio and review transaction history to stay on top of their financial activities."Username and Password to register, no sensitive information is required."
